I am currently a Research Scientist at [Fraunhofer IZFP](https://www.izfp.fraunhofer.de/en.html) and a PhD candidate in the [Laboratory of Vision and Digital Systems (LVSN)](https://vision.fsg.ulaval.ca/) at [Université Laval](https://www.ulaval.ca/en) under the supervision of [Prof. Xavier Maldague](https://xavier-maldague.fsg.ulaval.ca/en). 
I received my Master's degree from [RWTH Aachen University](https://www.rwth-aachen.de/cms/~a/root/?lidx=1) and my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Shanghai for Science and Technology. My research interests include **3D computer vision**, **vision-language models (VLMs)**, and **vision-language-action (VLA) models**.
